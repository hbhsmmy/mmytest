    Qingsui Sun

    Managing Director, Senior Portfolio Manager

    • 15 years of investment experience.
    • Previously, Ms. Sun was Director of Equity Investment Department of Bank of China Investment Management (BOCIM).
    • Ms. Sun is a top-down growth-oriented investor with proven capabilities in tactical asset allocation and thematic investment.
    • Under her management from 2007 to 2013, the BOCIM China Selected Hybrid Fund was ranked No.2 among 82 peers, outperforming Shanghai Composite Index by 103.4% (or 19.98% annually). And the fund was ranked among top 30% each year, regardless of bull or bear markets. The fund was rated by Morningstar and China Galaxy Securities Fund Research Center as both 3-year and 5-year Five-star mutual fund respectively.
    • Winner of 5-year Golden-Bull Award for three times.
    • Winner of 10-year Special Golden-Bull Award in China (only 7 equity portfolio managers have been awarded this special honor over the past 18 years).
