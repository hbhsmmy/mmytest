    Liwei Zhuo

    Chief Research Officer

    • 21 years of investment experience.
    • Previously Mr. Zhuo was Partner and Portfolio Manager of Greenwoods Asset Management.
    • Prior to Greenwoods, Mr. Zhuo was with CCB Principal Asset Management, progressively serving as sector analyst, portfolio manager, member of the investment committee and Deputy CIO. Under his management, the CCB Principal Eternal Value Equity Fund and the CCB Principal Income Enhanced Bond Fund have both achieved outstanding performance.
    • Mr. Zhuo is a long term believer and practitioner in rigorous fundamental research and corporate valuation. He seeks to invest in sustainable value along with high-quality growth, and favors companies with core competence that will consistently create value and have sound corporate governance in place.
    • Mr. Zhuo has developed a systematic investment framework in studying macro economics, industry dynamics and corporate fundamentals. He specializes in sector research in consumer goods, TMT, real estate and capital goods.
    • Winner of 3-Year Golden-Bull Award for Equity Hedge Fund in 2015.
    • In 2011, the CCB Principal Eternal Value Equity Fund managed by Mr. Zhuo received the 3-Year Five-star Rating by Galaxy Securities Fund Research Center.
