    Glen Qiu

    Chairman and Chief Executive Officer

    • In his 17 years' career, Mr. Qiu has held various positions including Partner of $6 billion asset management firm, Co-founding Partner of a global hedge fund, and Chief Investment Officer of a $47 billion mutual fund company.
    • Prior to joining Perseverance, Mr. Qiu was Chief Investment Officer and Chairman of investment committee of China Southern Asset Management, one of the largest mutual fund management companies in China. Previously, he was Portfolio Manager of Plinthos Capital, Partner of Altair Navigator Funds, and Partner of Wedge Capital Management.
    • Mr. Qiu is a deep value investor, striving to buy high quality companies at discounted prices.
    • Mr. Qiu has won various awards in recent years, including The Best CIO of the Year Award, The Most Influential People in Mutual Fund Investment Award, and The Best High-end Product in Mutual Fund Industry Award.
    • Mr. Qiu is the author of the No.1 best-selling investment book "The simple things in investment".
