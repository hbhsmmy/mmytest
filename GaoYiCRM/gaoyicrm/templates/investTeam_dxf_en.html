    Xiaofeng Deng

    Chief Investment Officer

    • 15 years of investment experience.
    • Previously, Mr. Deng was Managing Director of Equity Investment Department of Bosera Asset Management. He managed two products (Social Security Fund account and Bosera Theme Sector Fund) at the same time, both portfolios with AUM over RMB 10 billion and excellent performance under his management.
    • Mr. Deng is a bottom-up value investor, aiming to buy companies with good business models and top management team at very good prices. He has maintained a broad coverage of industries in China and has gained deep understanding through detailed research and investment for the past 15 years.
    • During his 9.5 years' management, the portfolio for Social Security Fund realized 1047% total return with annualized return of 29.1% .
    • Under his management, Bosera Theme Sector Fund (BTSF) was ranked No.1 among all equity mutual funds in terms of the accumulated total return over a period of 7 years and 10 months.
    • He made more than RMB 23.5 billion in investment gains for the investors from 2005 to 2014.
    • Winner of Golden-Bull Award for top portfolio managers in China for five times.
    • Winner of 10-year Special Golden-Bull Award in China (only 7 equity portfolio managers have been awarded this special honor over the past 18 years).
