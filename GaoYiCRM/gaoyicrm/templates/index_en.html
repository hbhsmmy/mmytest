    The Investment Team

    Glen Qiu

    Chairman

    Previously, Mr. Qiu was CIO and Chairman of Investment Committee of China Southern Asset Management, one of the largest asset management companies in China with $47 billion assets under management. His 17 years’ investment experience also includes Partner of $6 billion asset management firm and Co-founding Partner of a global hedge fund.

    Qingrui Sun

    Managing Director

    Previously, Ms. Sun was Director of Equity Investment Department of Bank of China Investment Management (BOCIM). Under her management from 2007 to 2013, the BOCIM China Selected Hybrid Fund was ranked No.2 among peers. She is winner of 10-year Special Golden-Bull Award in China.

    Xiaofeng Deng

    Chief Investment Officer

    Previously, Mr. Deng was Managing Director of Equity Investment Department of Bosera Asset Management. Under his management, Bosera Theme Sector Fund was ranked No.1 among peers over a period of 7 years and 10 months. During his 9.5 years’ management, the Social Security Fund portfolio achieved an annualized return of 29.1%. He is winner of 10-year Special Golden-Bull Award in China.

    Liwei Zhuo

    Chief Research Officer

    Previously, Mr. Zhuo was Partner and Portfolio Manager of Greenwoods Asset Management. Prior to that, he was Deputy CIO of CCB Principal Asset Management. He is winner of 3-year Golden-Bull Award for Equity Hedge Fund.

    Liu Feng

    Managing Director

    Mr. Feng has been an independent individual investor since 2003 with deep understanding of consumer, health care and retail industry. He is a long-term fundamental and contrarian investor, running a concentrated portfolio without any leverage.

    Simon Wang

    Managing Director

    Mr. Wang has 18 years of investment experience. Previously, he was a Director and a core member of the QFII team at Hillhouse Capital Group. Prior to that, he was an investment manager at Taikang Asset Management Co. Ltd.
